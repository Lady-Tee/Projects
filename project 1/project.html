ABOUT US

Treat yourself to the The Misha Hotel’s Toronto signature ONE Restaurant is a contemporary Yorkville hotspot helmed by Executive Chef Darby Piquette. The seasonal menu utilizes the finest quality local and international ingredients to offer globally inspired dishes that are flavour-forward. The sumptuous bar, tree-lined candle lit patio and lavish dining room make for an immersive dining experience day or night.

The Misha Hotel and ONE Restaurant offer a variety of private dining spaces that can be tailored to your specific event needs.
